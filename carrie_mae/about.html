About

Carrie Mae weems is known as a multi medium artist and as one of th emost influential contemporary American artists.. Her work embraces and develops from real life social issues from "family relationships, cultural identity, sexism, class, political systems, and the consequences of power." She is both a visual. Real life issues are reflected in her work by the emotion and energy that's conveyed in her work. Her work has been featured across the nation and she does both solo and group exhibitions.
