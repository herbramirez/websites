Case Study: SLAM

DESCRIPTION: For Emerging futures I explored SLAM technology. Simulatneously localization and mapping. Throught my findings it is a technology that's heavily used in the robotics industry. In short summary, "it is an agent location in repsonse to it's environgment."

Types There are many types SLAM is used and they range from topological, volumentric, known vs Unknown, static, single vs multiple, and active vs passive. All of these different methods and techniques use different components and strategies to localize and map different needs.

Systems

DESCRIPTION: For the systems map I mapped out different areas of interests for SLAM. The system covers key components such as where technology is being used, who are the main influencers and what type of agencies/institutions are using SLAM in what way.

SLAMin

DESCRIPTION: SLAMin is a microrobot pill that helps users map and localize all of their health issues by using an innovative SLAM algorythm. It's 2038 and humanity has overused this technology. In greedyness to beceome superhuman and super healthy, society failed to identify one main key component. The pills lifetime. After 18 years of use humans benefitted from all the information gathered by this technology to tackle future and current diseases. Now, after a few years of being disease/sickness free humans gave up on medicine study. The pills are now expiring and deteorating inside of every human that has used it leading to new sicknesses. As a result of the lack of study, humans are now dying

Museum A dedicated museum has been set up in Berlin. SLAMin is guarded by a greedy scientist who used the pill to become a giant. He is holed up in the fortress Berghain where guards artifacts and memories of the diseases SLAMin helped cure. For example, trench foot. As the years progressed and trench foot came back. Users can now walk over a giant iphone and view memories of what trenchfoot was once like. The other video depicts cuts, and how SLAM would tell you how to take care of an injury.
